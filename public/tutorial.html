Here is your very own Friendler Tricia! Treat it well and make sure to keep it happy and healthy by checking in to places with your friends!

How to Play

Earn XP by checking into public places like restaurants, parks, and other venues!

Bonus XP is rewarded if you check in with

  • friends (200 XP)
  • new friends (1000 XP)
  • multiple friends (200 XP/ friend)

Use your XP to:

  • level up your Friendler
  • purchase new equipment for your Friendler
  • feed your Friendler
